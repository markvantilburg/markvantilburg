## About BusinessMonitor

BusinessMonitor is an online research platform specialising in continuous evaluation for training providers, member organisations and service companies. The platform combines flexible survey design, advanced reporting and seamless CRM integration via API connections with leading providers, helping organisations shift their focus from routine data collection to continuous improvement.

Our latest development is **ImpactMonitor**, a standardised approach to measuring the return on investment in learning and development, with pilots currently underway and first results expected in 2026.

BusinessMonitor is a Dutch company with growing international ambitions, already serving clients beyond the Netherlands.
